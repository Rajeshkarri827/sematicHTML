About


Lambasingi, located in the Visakhapatnam district of Andhra Pradesh, is a hidden gem that boasts a unique climate and breathtaking scenery. This quaint village is situated at an altitude of approximately 1000 meters (3280 feet) above sea level, which contributes to its cool and pleasant weather throughout the year. The temperature in Lambasingi can drop to near freezing point in the winter, a rarity in southern India, and it occasionally experiences light snowfall, drawing visitors from all over the region. The village is enveloped by dense forests, which are home to diverse flora and fauna, making it a haven for nature enthusiasts. Coffee and pepper plantations are prevalent in the area, and the sight of these plantations adds to the charm of the landscape. Lambasingi is also known for its verdant valleys, mist-covered hills, and pristine waterfalls, such as Kothapalli Waterfalls, which is a popular attraction nearby. In addition to its natural beauty, Lambasingi offers opportunities for various outdoor activities like trekking, camping, and bird watching. The tranquil ambiance and the unspoiled environment provide a perfect setting for those looking to unwind and connect with nature.
